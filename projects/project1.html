Overview

This project involves using historical tourism data and predictive modeling techniques to estimate the number of tourists Morocco can expect for the World Cup 2030.

Data Collection

Data was collected from various sources including historical tourism statistics, economic indicators, and data from previous World Cup events.

Methodology

We used a combination of time series forecasting and regression analysis to build our predictive model. Various factors such as economic conditions and marketing efforts were taken into account.

Results

The model predicts that Morocco can expect approximately X million tourists during the World Cup 2030. This is based on historical trends and factors considered in the analysis.

Conclusion

This analysis provides valuable insights for stakeholders and helps in planning for the upcoming event. Further refinements can be made as more data becomes available.
